About Us

Ice Crown Cafe & Prints is a newly opened hub. The Cafe caters mostly students from nearby universities. Ice Crown Cafe were founded by a set of former students who wanted to help others by setting up a hub and a print shop in one. The cafe has a decent offering of meals, and beverages. On the otherhand, the print shop handles services like printing, laminating, binding, etc. The founders aims to make a change, and to offer more options and opportunities for students who wants to finish their schoolworks and to fill their tummies with good food.

Services

Ice Crown Cafe & Prints offers a wide variety of meals and beverages. It also offers printing services such as tarpaulin printing, document printing, binding, laminating, etc.
